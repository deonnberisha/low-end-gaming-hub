🛠️ Tips to Boost Gaming Performance

1. Close Background Apps

Shut down apps that use RAM while gaming to free up memory and improve FPS.

2. Update Graphics Drivers

Always keep your GPU drivers up to date to get the best performance.

3. Lower Game Resolution

Playing at 720p instead of 1080p can significantly increase FPS on weak PCs.

4. Use Game Performance Modes

Some games, like Fortnite or Valorant, have performance modes that reduce graphics for smoother gameplay.

5. Clean Temporary Files

Remove cache and temporary files regularly to prevent slowdown.

Bonus Tip: Use Cloud Gaming

If your PC still struggles, you can use services like GeForce NOW to play high-end games smoothly.
